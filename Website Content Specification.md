---

# CONTENT STYLE

Tone:

Professional

Honest

Clear

Confident

Never arrogant.

Never exaggerated.

Never marketing-like.

Never AI sounding.

---

# WRITING RULES

Use short paragraphs.

Avoid buzzwords.

Avoid generic motivational language.

Every sentence should provide information.

No filler.

No clichés.

No meaningless adjectives.
